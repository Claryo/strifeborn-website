# STRIFEBORN — BLESSING KITS v6

> Blessings are acquired during runs after Swarm waves. Each blessing belongs to a **Category** (Economy, Player, Teamwide) and a **Rarity** (Bronze, Silver, Gold). Higher rarity blessings are rarer but more impactful.
> By default, each blessing screen offers 3 choices and 2 rerolls.
> By default, each hero recruitment screen offers 5 choices and 3 rerolls at game start, 2 rerolls after.
> By default, each shop screen offers a set of items, objects, synergies and heroes, and 2 rerolls.

> **General reminders:**
> - Player Life: 5 by default. Each defeat removes exactly 1 life.
> - Hero spawn costs 3 gold by default. Hero spawn places a hero from your pool onto the bench at Tier 1 by default.
> - Hero recruitment (adding a hero to your pool) is free with a set number of rerolls. Rerolls on shop, blessing, and hero recruitment screens are always count-based, never gold-costed.
> - Deploy Slots: 3 by default, max 12. Increasing costs 5 → 10 → 15 → 20 → 25 gold (cap at 25).
> - Shop Level: determines the quality and rarity distribution of available offers.
> - Frontline: rows 1–2 of the Player Area. Backline: rows 3–4 of the Player Area.
> - All objects (Hero Reroll, Item Reroll, Hero Duplication, Item Duplication, Hero Tier Enhancer, Unfuse, Small Forge, Advanced Forge, Relic Forge) go to the bench or overflow when granted and must be used manually.
> - Hero Reroll object: drag onto a hero to reroll them into a random hero from your pool at the same tier.
> - Item Reroll object: drag onto an item to reroll it into a random item of the same rarity, or onto an artifact to get a random artifact from your pool.
> - Hero Duplication object: drag onto a hero to duplicate them at Tier -1 (minimum Tier 1).
> - Item Duplication object: drag onto a basic item to duplicate it. Does not work on advanced items, hero relics, or artifacts.
> - Hero Tier Enhancer object: drag onto a hero to grant +1 tier. Bronze applicable if hero tier ≤ 2, Silver if tier ≤ 4, Gold if tier ≤ 6.
> - Unfuse object: drag onto an advanced item to split it back into its 2 basic component items.
> - Small Forge: tap on the object to let the player choose 1 basic item amongst 3.
> - Advanced Forge: tap on the object to let the player choose 1 advanced item amongst 3.
> - Relic Forge: tap on the object to let the player choose 1 hero relic amongst 3.
> - Sell value of a hero: 2^(hero tier) gold.
> - **Sell safety rule:** Sell value bonuses from blessings never apply to Tier 1 heroes. A Tier 1 hero always sells for exactly 2 gold.

## ECONOMY

### Bronze

**Startup Fund**
Gain 3 gold immediately. At the start of each Act, gain an additional 3 gold.

**Stockpile I**
At the start of each preparation phase, if your bench has 10 or more occupied slots, gain +2 gold.
*Play: hoard your bench — resist the urge to sell everything.*

**Salvage I**
Selling a Tier 2+ hero grants +1 bonus gold.
*Play: tier heroes up, then sell for profit — T1 cycling won't work.*

**Bounty I**
Gain +2 gold after Elite waves and +2 gold after Swarm waves (on top of base rewards).

**Frontline Wages**
At the end of each victorious wave, gain +1 gold per hero deployed in frontline (rows 1–2) that survived.
*Play: stack frontline and keep them alive.*

**Merge Profit**
Gain +1 gold each time you complete a hero merge.
*Play: merge often — buy duplicates even when you don't need the hero.*

**Quick Flip**
The first Tier 2+ hero sold each preparation phase grants double sell value.

**Bargain Hunter**
The first 2 hero spawns each preparation phase cost 1 less gold (minimum 1).

**Bruised Pockets**
Gain +3 gold at the start of each preparation phase after a defeat. No bonus after victories.
*Play: losing pays — pivot after defeats instead of panicking.*

**Full Deploy**
Gain +2 gold at the end of each wave where all your Deploy Slots are filled.
*Play: fill every slot — an empty slot is wasted gold. Pushes you to invest in deploy capacity AND fill it.*

---

### Silver

**Stockpile II**
At the start of each preparation phase, if your bench has 10 or more occupied slots, gain +4 gold and 1 Small Forge object.
*Play: your bench IS your income — never sell unless you must.*

**Salvage II**
Tier 2+ heroes sell for +50% bonus gold. Items and artifacts sell for +50% bonus gold.
*Play: everything is liquid — tier up, rebuild, sell fearlessly.*

**Bounty II**
Gain +4 gold after Elite waves and +3 gold after Swarm waves (on top of base rewards).

**Backline Investment**
At the end of each victorious wave, gain +2 gold per hero deployed in backline (rows 3–4) that survived.
*Play: protect your backline — they're your money makers.*

**Economic Momentum**
Gain +1 bonus gold per wave for each consecutive victory (resets on defeat, max +5).
*Play: never lose — or lose everything built up.*

**Loan Shark**
Gain 20 gold immediately. Lose 2 gold per wave for the next 5 waves.
*Play: power-spike NOW and pray it pays off before debt ends.*

**Black Market**
Shop Level +1. Each shop also offers 1 additional item slot with a guaranteed advanced item.

**Merge Dividends**
Twice per preparation phase, when you complete a hero merge, gain +3 gold and 1 random basic item.
*Play: merge is your economy engine — buy duplicates constantly, merge for profit AND power.*

**Golden Harvest**
Gain +3 bonus gold after every victorious battle.

**Tight Roster**
Gain +3 gold per wave for each empty Deploy Slot (slots you could fill but choose not to).
*Play: deploy FEWER heroes on purpose — quality over quantity.*

---

### Gold

**Stockpile III**
At the start of each preparation phase, if your bench has 10 or more occupied slots, gain +6 gold and 1 Advanced Forge object.
*Play: the bench is sacred — guard every slot.*

**Salvage III**
Selling a Tier 4+ hero refunds its full invested gold value (ignores normal sell formula). All other Tier 2+ sell values +50%.
*Play: tier-up heroes just to sell them — the economy IS your weapon.*

**Bounty III**
Gain +6 gold after Elite waves and +5 gold after Swarm waves. After each Elite wave, gain 1 Advanced Forge object.

**Speculator**
After each wave, gain gold equal to 20% of your current gold (max +10 per wave).
*Play: hoard gold — the more you sit on, the more you earn. Every purchase hurts.*

**Chorus Patronage**
Whenever you select a Blessing, gain +5 gold.

**Golden Reserves**
Gain +2 gold per wave for each Gold-rarity blessing you own (including this one).
*Play: chase Gold blessings above all else.*

**Fortune's Favor**
After each victorious battle, choose: take the normal gold reward, or gamble — flip for double or nothing.

**Midas Touch**
Every 4th hero spawn is free.

**War Economy**
Each time you win a wave, gain +3 bonus gold per hero Tier 4+ on the battlefield.
*Play: invest hard into a few high-tier carries — they pay for themselves.*

**Golden Empire**
Gain +5 gold at the start of each wave. Each consecutive victory grants +1 additional gold (max +5 bonus). Each defeat resets the bonus to 0 AND costs 3 gold.
*Play: winning is everything — defeat is financially devastating.*

## PLAYER

### Bronze

**Tactical Expansion**
+1 Deploy Slot.

**Forge I**
Gain 1 Small Forge object.

**Promotion I**
Gain 1 Bronze Hero Tier Enhancer object.

**Synergy Gift**
Gain 1 random synergy token.

**Scavenger**
When you lose a battle, gain 1 random basic item.
*Play: defeat gives resources — treat losses as investment.*

**Reinforcement**
Gain 1 Hero Duplication object.

**Reforge Kit**
Gain 3 Item Reroll objects.

**Field Training**
For the next 2 waves, the first 2 hero spawns each preparation phase arrive at +1 Tier.

**Vanguard I**
At the end of each wave, each hero deployed in frontline (rows 1–2) that survived gains +2% Max HP permanently.
*Play: keep your frontline alive — they grow stronger each wave.*

**Recall Order**
Gain 2 Hero Reroll objects.

---

### Silver

**Forge II**
Gain 1 Advanced Forge object.

**Promotion II**
Gain 1 Silver Hero Tier Enhancer object. After each Act, gain 1 Bronze Hero Tier Enhancer object.

**Synergy Recruit**
Gain 1 random artifact and 1 hero that shares a synergy with that artifact.
*Play: instant composition building block — adapt around what you receive.*

**Second Wind**
After losing a battle, your highest-tier deployed hero permanently gains +1 Tier. Triggers up to 3 times per run.

**Adaptive Strategy**
Blessing screens offer 1 additional choice for the run.

**Tactical Promotion**
For the next 4 waves, after each battle, 1 random deployed hero gains +1 Tier.

**Reserve Corps**
Heroes on the bench gain +1% to all stats per wave (stacks permanently, applies when deployed).
*Play: bench heroes early, deploy them late — patience is power.*

**Vanguard II**
At the end of each wave, each hero in frontline (rows 1–2) that survived gains +3% to all stats permanently. Each hero in backline (rows 3–4) that survived gains +2% Strength and +2% Wisdom permanently.
*Play: positioning = permanent growth. Move carefully, commit to rows.*

**Chain of Command**
Each time a hero reaches Tier 4+, gain 1 Hero Duplication object and +10 gold.
*Play: funnel tiers into carries — each one reaching T4 funds the next.*

**Champion's Focus**
Choose 1 deployed hero. That hero gains +10% to all stats permanently. All other deployed heroes lose -3% to all stats permanently.
*Play: pick your carry — everyone else sacrifices for them.*

---

### Gold

**Forge III**
Gain 1 Relic Forge object immediately. After each Act, gain 1 Advanced Forge object.

**Promotion III**
At the start of each Act, choose 1 deployed hero to permanently gain +2 Tiers.
*Play: commit to a carry — pour everything into one hero per Act.*

**Patron of Synergies**
Gain 2 random artifacts. For each, also gain 1 hero that shares a synergy with that artifact.
*Play: instant synergy injection — reshape your comp around what arrives.*

**Fate's Gambit**
Double all gold gains. Immediately lose 2 Player Lives.
*Play: you're rich and fragile — spend fast, build strong, don't lose.*

**Iron Bastion**
All hero merges have a 25% chance to grant +1 additional Tier. All allies start battle with -10% Strength and -10% Wisdom.
*Play: merge relentlessly — the bonus tiers offset the stat penalty and then some.*

**Ascended Army**
For the next 4 waves, after each battle, 2 random deployed heroes gain +1 Tier. All allies start battle with -10% Max HP.
*Play: ride the power spike window — push hard before the penalty catches up.*

**War Council**
After each Elite wave, choose 1 of 3 Gold-rarity Blessings as a bonus selection (does not replace normal Blessing screens).

**Vanguard III**
At the end of each wave, each hero in frontline (rows 1–2) that survived gains +5% to all stats permanently (max +50% per hero). Each hero in backline (rows 3–4) that survived gains +3% to all stats permanently (max +30% per hero).
*Play: every surviving hero is an investment — protect the board at all costs. Growth is massive but capped.*

**Masterwork**
Each time a hero reaches Tier 5+, gain 1 Advanced Forge object. Each time a hero reaches Tier 7, gain 1 Relic Forge object.

**Final Stand**
The first time you would reach 0 lives, survive at 1 life. For the remainder of the run, all allies gain +35% Damage Amplification and +25% Attack Speed.

## TEAMWIDE

### Bronze

**Battle Cry**
All allies gain +20% Strength, +20% Wisdom and 20% Attack Speed for the first 5 seconds of battle.

**Hardened Armor**
All allies gain +40 Physical Defense and +40 Spell Defense.

**Shared Vigor**
All allies gain +8% Max HP.

**Quick Draw**
All allies gain +10% Attack Speed.

**Focused Mind**
All allies start battle with bonus Mana equal to 10% of their Max Mana, added on top of their Starting Mana.
*Reminder: Starting Mana is a discrete value per hero (0%, 25%, 50%, 75%, or 100% of Max Mana). This bonus is additive and not subject to discrete steps.*

**Formation I**
All allies adjacent to at least 1 other ally gain +5% Damage Reduction.
*Play: cluster your heroes — isolation is weakness.*

**Frontline I**
Allies in frontline (rows 1–2) gain +15% Max HP.

**Backline I**
Allies in backline (rows 3–4) gain +20% Attack Speed.

**Lone Wolf**
Allies not adjacent to any other ally gain +15% Strength and +15% Wisdom.
*Play: spread your heroes apart — each one fights alone but harder.*

**Sharp Instinct**
All allies gain +5% Crit Chance.

---

### Silver

**Bloodlust**
When an ally kills an enemy, they gain +20% Attack Speed and +5% Lifesteal for 4 seconds.

**Critical Mass**
All allies gain +8% Crit Chance and +20% Crit Damage.

**Mana Flow**
All allies gain +5 bonus Mana per Basic Attack hit.

**Arcane Surge**
Each ally's first Spell cast per battle deals +30% bonus damage.

**Relentless Assault**
All allies gain +15% Attack Speed.

**Formation II**
All allies adjacent to at least 2 other allies gain +10% Damage Reduction and +10% Strength.
*Play: tight cluster — adjacency is power, but AoE will punish you.*

**Frontline II**
Allies in frontline (rows 1–2) gain +25% Max HP and +60 Physical Defense.

**Backline II**
Allies in backline (rows 3–4) gain +30% Attack Speed, +10% Wisdom and +10% Strength.

**Pack Hunters**
All allies deal +3% bonus damage for each other ally currently alive on the battlefield.
*Play: keep everyone alive — each death weakens the whole team.*

**Adrenaline Rush**
When an ally drops below 40% HP for the first time per battle, they gain +30% Attack Speed and +10% Lifesteal for 4 seconds.

---

### Gold

**Titan's Blessing**
All allies gain +20% Max HP, +20% Strength, and +20% Wisdom. All allies' Attack Speed reduced by 25%.
*Play: slow, heavy, devastating hits — build around burst spells and big crits, not sustained DPS.*

**Avatar of War**
All allies' Basic Attacks deal bonus Pure Damage equal to 15% of their Strength. All allies' Max Mana increased by 40%.
*Play: spells come slower but auto-attacks shred — build physical carries, ignore spell-dependent heroes.*

**Glass Cannon**
All allies gain +35% Strength and +40% Crit Damage. All allies lose -20% Max HP.
*Play: kill them before they kill you. One bad round and you're dead.*

**Synchrony**
All active synergies count as having +1 additional unit toward their thresholds. All allies start battle with -15% Strength and -15% Wisdom.
*Play: hit synergy breakpoints with fewer heroes — but each hero hits softer.*

**Ascension**
All allies gain +1% Strength, +1% Wisdom, and +1% Max HP per second during battle (resets each battle). All allies start battle at 50% Max HP.
*Play: survive the opening and you become unstoppable — frontline must hold or everything collapses.*

**Formation III**
All allies adjacent to at least 2 other allies gain +20% Damage Reduction, +15% Strength, and +15% Wisdom. Allies not adjacent to any other ally take +20% more damage.
*Play: cluster or die. Devastating with tight comps, suicidal for spread formations.*

**Frontline III**
Allies in frontline (rows 1–2) gain +40% Max HP, +90 Physical Defense, and +60 Spell Defense. Allies in backline (rows 3–4) lose -15% Strength and -15% Wisdom.
*Play: your frontline becomes an impenetrable wall. Your backline becomes weak.*

**Backline III**
Allies in backline (rows 3–4) gain +40% Attack Speed, +25% Wisdom, and +25% Strength. Allies in frontline (rows 1–2) lose -20% Max HP.
*Play: your backline becomes strong. Your frontline becomes too weak to protect.*

**Undying Flame**
When an ally dies, all remaining allies gain +15% Strength, +15% Wisdom, and +15% Attack Speed for 5 seconds (stacks with each death).
*Play: sacrifice weak units on purpose — your carries feast on death.*

**Spell Overload**
All allies gain +35% Wisdom and +200% bonus Mana per Basic Attack hit. All allies lose -40% Attack Speed.
*Play: spells fly constantly, but everyone is slow. Build caster-heavy and pray for fast kills.*
