# Meta Progression & Out-of-Run Systems

This section defines all systems that persist **outside of runs** in Strifeborn.

Meta progression exists to:

* Reward long-term engagement
* Expand strategic options
* Express mastery and identity

Meta progression must **never invalidate run-time skill or decision-making**.

## 16.1 Meta Design Philosophy

Meta progression in Strifeborn is **hybrid**, with strict boundaries.

It is designed to:

* Add **options**, not mandatory power
* Unlock content and customization
* Provide minor, flavorful progression

Meta progression must:

* Preserve competitive integrity
* Remain readable and optional
* Avoid pay-to-win dynamics

## 16.2 Meta Currencies

Strifeborn uses a **two-tier meta economy**, with additional task-specific currencies.

### 16.2.1 Valor

Valor is the **primary meta currency**.

Used for:

* Unlocking heroes
* Leveling heroes
* Upgrading persistent equipment
* Improving hero relics
* Unlocking meta augments
* General progression systems

Valor is earned through:

* Playing any game mode
* Campaign progression
* Challenges and achievements

### 16.2.2 Dissonance *(Premium Currency)*

Dissonance is a **premium meta currency**.

Primary uses:

* Cosmetics
* Skins
* Visual effects
* Optional progression acceleration

Dissonance:

* Can substitute Valor for some actions
* Never provides exclusive power
* Is never required to compete

Free-to-play players can fully access competitive systems.

### 16.2.3 Minor Meta Currencies

Additional currencies may exist for specific purposes, such as:

* Hero leveling materials
* Equipment tuning
* Relic upgrades
* Cosmetic unlocks

These currencies:

* Serve targeted progression loops
* Never gate core gameplay systems

## 16.3 Hero Unlocking

Heroes are unlocked through:

* **Campaign progression**
* **Achievements and challenges**

Hero unlocking:

* Is deterministic
* Is never random
* Encourages exploration of different modes

All heroes are designed to be obtainable without premium currency.

## 16.4 Hero Leveling (Out-of-Run)

Heroes gain **experience by being played**.

**Hero Levels**

* Levels are shared across all modes
* Levels persist between runs

Hero levels grant:

* **Minor, flavorful stat bonuses**, unique per hero
* Unlock access to the hero’s **Relic**
* Unlock additional **persistent equipment slots** (up to 5 total)
* Periodic **cosmetics or mastery displays**

Hero leveling:

* Must not break hero balance
* Must not redefine hero roles
* Reinforces identity rather than raw power

## 16.5 Persistent Hero Equipment

Persistent hero equipment exists outside of runs.

* Equipment is configured **before starting a run**
* Equipment modifies how heroes behave during runs
* Equipment is limited by unlocked slots

Persistent equipment:

* Is upgraded using Valor and minor currencies
* Must follow strict power budgets
* Cannot invalidate run-time itemization

## 16.6 Hero Relics as Meta–Run Bridge

Hero Relics are the **primary link between meta and run systems**.

* Relics are upgraded out-of-run
* Relics appear and are used during runs
* Relic upgrades enhance identity, not raw stats

Relics:

* Are never mandatory
* Are never exclusive to premium currency
* Preserve run-time decision importance

## 16.7 Meta Augments

Meta augments provide **horizontal progression**.

They may:

* Expand player options
* Add flexibility to systems
* Modify starting conditions slightly

Meta augments:

* Never grant raw combat stats
* Never affect enemies
* Never override core systems

Examples include:

* Additional rerolls
* Extra choices
* Alternative system interactions

## 16.8 PvP Meta Rules

In PvP modes:

* Hero levels are **enabled**
* Persistent equipment is **enabled**
* Relics are **enabled**

PvP balance is preserved because:

* Meta bonuses are minor
* Power differences remain limited
* Leaderboards naturally reflect experience and mastery

There is no hard normalization, but no system creates insurmountable advantages.

## 16.9 Hard Prohibitions

Meta progression must **never**:

* Modify enemy stats
* Alter wave difficulty curves
* Change blessing rarity rules
* Break economy constraints

Any new meta system must respect these boundaries.

## 16.10 Separation of Concerns

This section defines:

* Persistent progression systems
* Meta currencies
* Unlock structures

The following are defined elsewhere:

* Numerical tuning
* Individual upgrades
* Cosmetic catalogs
* UI implementation
