# Characters Sprite Style

The silhouette is compact and disciplined, shown in a neutral 3/4 idle pose with slight chibi proportions. Rendered as cartoon-style pixel art with thick outlines, compact proportions and transparent background. 32 limited color palette

# Aegis

**Swarm Enemies — Disposable Infantry**

**Common visual description (Swarm)**

A frail dieselpunk infantry soldier wearing a cheap industrial gas mask with round glass lenses and a dangling rubber hose, completely hiding the face. The uniform is thin, ill-fitted and clearly mass-produced, with wrinkled fabric, exposed stitching and little protection. Armor is minimal and improvised, made of small riveted metal plates bolted directly onto the chest or shoulders. Straps, belts and cables hang loosely, some slightly misaligned, reinforcing a rushed and expendable look. The overall silhouette is narrow and fragile, immediately communicating a weak, disposable frontline unit meant to overwhelm through numbers rather than strength.

**Equipment / weapon specificities (Swarm)**

* **Shield unit**  
  Small rectangular riot shield made of thin industrial metal, heavily scratched and dented, paired with a short baton or crude melee weapon.
* **Bayonet unit**  
  Long bolt-action rifle fitted with a fixed bayonet, visibly oversized for the soldier, emphasizing desperation and low survivability.
* **Rifle unit**  
  Basic bolt-action rifle with no attachments, worn sling, and simple iron sights, clearly outdated and cheaply manufactured.

**Normal Enemies — Industrial Line Troopers (5 Units)**

**Common visual description (Normal)**

A disciplined dieselpunk infantry soldier wearing a reinforced military gas mask with angular metal framing, thick hoses and integrated filters, completely obscuring the face. The uniform is properly fitted and standardized, made of heavy fabric with reinforced joints and visible wear from repeated deployments. Armor plates are deliberately mounted on the chest, shoulders and thighs, secured with rivets and welds, showing industrial mass production rather than individuality. Ammunition pouches, utility gear and cables are neatly arranged and symmetrical, conveying training, discipline and battlefield experience. The silhouette is broad, stable and confident, clearly distinct from disposable infantry and identifying a competent frontline combatant.

**Normal Enemy Variants (Names + Equipment)**

* **Bulwark Trooper**  
  Large reinforced ballistic shield with a narrow viewing slit, paired with a compact sidearm or short defensive weapon.
* **Rifle Trooper**  
  Standardized military rifle with reinforced stock, visible magazine and sturdier barrel assembly, clearly superior to swarm weapons.
* **Suppressor Trooper**  
  Heavy automatic weapon or light machine gun with a box magazine or belt feed, thick barrel and reinforced grip, built for sustained fire.
* **Shock Trooper**  
  Short-range weapon such as a compact shotgun, submachine gun or industrial breaching firearm, designed for aggressive frontline pressure.
* **Grenadier Trooper**  
  Rifle or launcher equipped with an under-barrel grenade system or visible explosive canisters, emphasizing area denial and indirect pressure.

# Eclipse

**Swarm Enemies — Disposable Fanatics**

**Common visual description (Swarm)**

A lightly protected humanoid fanatic wearing a crude ritual mask made of bone, cracked metal or stitched fabric, fully obscuring the face except for glowing, aggressive eyes. The body is lean and exposed, wrapped in torn straps, bandages and scavenged armor fragments that barely offer protection. Clothing is asymmetrical, damaged and loosely secured, with visible skin and muscle, reinforcing a reckless and disposable nature. The silhouette is narrow and unstable, conveying a weak but relentless attacker driven by aggression rather than survival or discipline.

**Equipment / weapon specificities (Swarm)**

* **Unarmed fanatic**  
  Bare hands, claws or crude knuckle blades, with bandaged forearms and exposed flesh.
* **Cleaver fanatic**  
  Large rusted cleaver or hooked blade, oversized and poorly balanced.
* **Hunting fanatic**  
  Short improvised ranged weapon such as a crude firearm, crossbow or thrown blades.

**Normal Enemies — Hardened Cult Fighters (5 Units)**

**Common visual description (Normal)**

A hardened humanoid cult fighter wearing a reinforced ritual mask with angular shapes, metal inlays and glowing eye slits, fully concealing the face while projecting menace and control. The armor is layered and deliberate, combining industrial metal plates with reinforced leather or heavy fabric beneath, marked by ritual engravings and signs of repeated repair. Gear is tightly strapped and well-maintained, showing experience rather than decay. Spikes, symbols or corrupted conduits are integrated in a functional and restrained way. The silhouette is broader, heavier and more grounded than swarm units, clearly communicating a trained, mass-produced but dangerous combatant.

**Normal Enemy Variants (Names + Equipment)**

* **Bulwark Ravager**  
  Heavy corrupted shield reinforced with ritual plating, paired with a short brutal melee weapon for close control.
* **Execution Ravager**  
  Large two-handed melee weapon such as a cleaver, hammer or scythe-like blade, built for raw killing power.
* **Stalker Ravager**  
  Reliable mid-range weapon such as a compact rifle, crossbow or stabilized corrupted firearm, designed for pursuit and pressure.
* **Hex Ravager**  
  One-handed weapon combined with a corrupted focus, totem or energy-emitting device integrated into the armor.
* **Reaver Ravager**  
  Long-reaching polearm or chain-based weapon, designed to punish positioning and control space.

# Inquisition

**Swarm Enemies — Penitent Rabble**

**Common visual description (Swarm)**

A frail theocratic zealot wearing a crude devotional hood or stitched mask reinforced with thin metal scraps, fully obscuring the face except for narrow eye slits or faint glowing symbols. The body is lightly protected, wrapped in simple robes, torn tabards or stitched cloth covered in prayers and purity markings. Armor is minimal and poorly fitted, limited to small shoulder plates or chest fragments held together by rope or chains. Religious seals, parchment strips and censers hang loosely from the body, swaying with movement and emphasizing instability. The silhouette is narrow, hunched and submissive, clearly communicating a weak, disposable unit driven by blind faith rather than training or survival.

**Swarm Enemy Variants (Names + Equipment)**

* **Flagbearer Rabble**  
  Carries a tall devotional banner, icon or standard mounted on a pole, sometimes reinforced with metal edges and used as a crude melee weapon.
* **Fervent Rabble**  
  Wields a short ritual melee weapon such as a club, dagger or sacrificial blade, often chained to the arm alongside a scripture or reliquary.
* **Watchful Rabble**  
  Uses a simple ranged weapon such as a light crossbow, sling or crude firearm, lacking stabilization and clearly inferior to normal troops.

**Normal Enemies — Zealous Inquisition Penitents (5 Units)**

**Common visual description (Normal)**

A masked theocratic soldier wearing a severe ceremonial helm or metal face mask engraved with religious symbols, scripture marks or purity seals, fully concealing the face. The armor is heavy and deliberately restrictive, combining layered metal plates with reinforced robes, tabards or chain elements beneath. Straps, clasps and bindings are tight and symmetrical, suggesting ritual discipline and enforced obedience rather than comfort. Incense holders, parchment strips, seals or reliquary fragments are integrated into the gear in a controlled and standardized way. The silhouette is upright, rigid and imposing, clearly communicating a trained, mass-produced enforcer driven by faith and punishment rather than individuality.

**Normal Enemy Variants (Names + Equipment)**

* **Bulwark Penitent**  
  Large sanctified shield engraved with religious symbols, paired with a one-handed mace or flail designed for defensive control.
* **Judicator Penitent**  
  One-handed melee weapon such as a mace, hammer or sword combined with a holy focus or scripture-bound relic integrated into the armor.
* **Purifier Penitent**  
  Short-range cleansing weapon such as a flamer, censer-launcher or chemical sprayer, with visible fuel tanks or canisters.
* **Marksman Penitent**  
  Long-range firearm such as a rifle or crossbow fitted with purity seals, prayer scrolls or ritual markings for sanctioned execution.
* **Flagellant Penitent**  
  Chain-based or whip-like weapon, flail or scourge, often wrapped around the arm or waist, designed to punish positioning and enforce control.

# Nebulae

**Swarm Enemies — Void Fragments**

**Common visual description (Swarm)**

A small, unstable alien construct formed from floating crystalline shards and semi-translucent matter, loosely held together by faint energy fields. The body lacks a clear humanoid structure, instead forming an asymmetrical mass of jagged fragments with glowing cores or veins of light running through them. Surfaces appear brittle, cracked and uneven, as if constantly on the verge of breaking apart. Pieces drift slightly out of alignment, reinforcing an impression of fragility and impermanence. The silhouette is light, erratic and irregular, clearly communicating a weak, disposable entity driven forward in large numbers rather than individual strength.

**Swarm Enemy Variants (Names + Equipment / Form)**

* **Crystalline Fragment**  
  Compact shard cluster with blunt protrusions, colliding directly with targets through raw kinetic impact.
* **Claw Fragment**  
  Fragment cluster shaped with elongated, blade-like extensions, forming crude grasping or slashing limbs.
* **Drifting Fragment**  
  Lighter floating fragment with a glowing core that emits short-range energy pulses or weak ranged strikes.

**Normal Enemies — Void Constructs (5 Units)**

**Common visual description (Normal)**

A stabilized alien construct composed of interlocked crystalline plates and dense energy cores, forming a deliberate and controlled silhouette. The structure is symmetrical and reinforced, with sharp geometric edges and layered segments that lock together like engineered armor. Glowing lines of energy run through the body in precise patterns, suggesting internal regulation and purpose. Unlike swarm entities, these constructs hold their form rigidly, projecting weight, control and durability. The overall shape is taller, heavier and more imposing, clearly identifying an experienced, mass-produced combat unit rather than a disposable fragment.

**Normal Enemy Variants (Names + Form / Weapon)**

* **Bulwark Construct**  
  Dense frontal plating forming a shield-like mass, with energy fields reinforcing the forward-facing surface.
* **Reaper Construct**  
  Elongated bladed limbs or rotating shard-arms, designed for sustained close-range cutting attacks.
* **Lancer Construct**  
  Long, spear-like crystalline projection extending from the body, used for precise mid-range thrusts or energy lances.
* **Emitter Construct**  
  Centralized glowing core with focusing crystals, capable of controlled ranged energy discharges.
* **Warden Construct**  
  Heavy, compact build with multiple interlocking plates and stabilizers, projecting control and area denial through mass and presence.

# Shogunate

**Swarm Enemies — Shogunate Levies**

**Common visual description (Swarm)**

A lightly equipped feudal cyberpunk foot soldier wearing a crude half-mask or simple metal faceplate, partially concealing the face while leaving parts exposed. Clothing consists of thin layered fabric, short robes or worn uniforms mixed with low-quality cybernetic fittings. Armor is minimal and poorly fitted, limited to small shoulder guards or chest scraps loosely tied with cords. Exposed cables, cheap augmentations and uneven plating suggest rushed mass production and low survivability. The silhouette is narrow and slightly hunched, clearly communicating a weak, disposable unit intended to overwhelm through numbers rather than skill.

**Swarm Enemy Variants (Names + Equipment)**

* **Gun Levy**
