# STRIFEBORN — FACTION OATHS v5

> Faction Oaths are **run modifiers** selected at the start of a run. Each oath fundamentally shapes how the player approaches core systems. A player picks **one oath** from the pool of the faction they choose.
> Every oath has a meaningful tradeoff. There are no free lunches.
> By default, each blessing screen offers 3 choices and 2 rerolls.
> By default, each hero recruitment screen offers 5 choices and 3 rerolls at game start, 2 rerolls after.
> By default, each shop screen offers a set of items, objects, synergies and heroes, and 2 rerolls.

> **General reminders:**
> - Player Life: 5 by default. Each defeat removes exactly 1 life.
> - Hero spawn costs 3 gold by default. Hero spawn places a hero from your pool onto the bench at Tier 1 by default.
> - Hero recruitment (adding a hero to your pool) is free with a set number of rerolls. Rerolls on shop, blessing, and hero recruitment screens are always count-based, never gold-costed.
> - Deploy Slots: 3 by default, max 12. Increasing costs 5 → 10 → 15 → 20 → 25 gold (cap at 25).
> - Shop Level: determines the quality and rarity distribution of available offers.
> - Frontline: rows 1–2 of the Player Area. Backline: rows 3–4 of the Player Area.
> - Sell value of a hero: 2^(hero tier) gold.
> - All objects (Hero Reroll, Item Reroll, Hero Duplication, Item Duplication, Hero Tier Enhancer, Unfuse, Small Forge, Advanced Forge, Relic Forge) go to the bench or overflow when granted and must be used manually.
> - Meta currencies: Valor (primary), Hero Medals (per-hero leveling), Equipment Chests (5 rarity levels).

## AEGIS
*Identity: Shields, protection, endurance, frontline fortification.*
*Playstyle: hold the line, commit to your heroes, outlast everything.*

### Oath of the Bulwark
All allies deployed in frontline (rows 1–2) start each battle with a Shield equal to 25% of their Max HP. Allies deployed in backline (rows 3–4) start battle with -10% Max HP.
*Play: stack your frontline heavy — your backline is exposed, so your wall MUST hold.*

### Oath of the Guardian
Shields on all allies are 50% stronger. All healing received by all allies is reduced by 50%.
*Play: build around shields, not sustain. Lifesteal and healing become near-useless — you live and die by your shield generation.*

### Oath of Fortification
Heroes gain +5% to all stats for each consecutive wave they remain deployed on the battlefield (stacks permanently, resets if the hero is removed). Hero spawn cost +2 gold.
*Play: never swap heroes. Your army is expensive to build but grows monstrous if you stay loyal. Pivoting is punished — commit early.*

### Oath of Endurance
Start with +2 Player Lives (7 total). After each victory, gain +1 bonus gold per remaining Player Life.
*Play: your lives ARE your economy. 7 lives = +7g per victory. Every loss permanently bleeds gold for the rest of the run. You can afford to lose — but you'll feel every single one in your wallet. Never. Lose.*

### Oath of the Iron Wall
Max Deploy Slots reduced by 3 (max 9 instead of 12). All allies gain +15% to all stats. At the end of the run, gain +30% bonus Hero Medals for all heroes used.
*Play: fewer heroes, all stronger. You'll never have a wide board — build a compact, elite squad. Bonus medals reward the grind.*

## ECLIPSE
*Identity: Death-fueled power, darkness, revival, harvest from carnage.*
*Playstyle: embrace death, profit from chaos, turn loss into fuel.*

### Oath of the Reborn
All allies revive once per battle at 20% HP with +25% Damage Amplification for 3 seconds. All allies start battle with -20% Max HP.
*Play: your heroes WILL die — that's the plan. Build around the revive burst: glass cannon carries that explode after coming back.*

### Oath of Harvest
On enemy kill during battle, gain +1 gold (max +5 per battle). Winning a wave without losing any ally grants +3 bonus gold. Losing a wave consumes 2 Player Lives instead of 1.
*Play: perfect play is massively rewarded — but each loss costs double. High skill, high stakes, high gold.*

### Oath of Shadow
Start the run with -4 Player Lives (1 total) and +25 gold. After every Act, gain +1 Player Life and 1 Advanced Forge object. Every 3 victories, gain 1 Hero Duplication object.
*Play: the most dangerous opening in the game. One loss and you're dead — but the gold head start and scaling rewards are enormous. Survive the early game and you snowball.*

### Oath of Carnage
All allies gain +25% Damage Amplification. All allies take +20% more damage. Each defeat costs 1 additional Player Life.
*Play: everything dies faster — including your team. Fights are brutal and short. You can't afford to lose, ever. Build overwhelming offense.*

### Oath of the Eclipse
Blessing screens always show Gold rarity blessings. Each blessing screen offers only 1 choice (no rerolls). Start the run with -1 Player Life (4 total).
*Play: guaranteed Gold blessings but zero control. You get what you get and build around it. The run is shaped by fate — your job is to adapt.*

## INQUISITION
*Identity: Holy magic, wisdom, judgment, marking targets, casting power.*
*Playstyle: accumulate knowledge, pick the right tools, empower through choices.*

### Oath of the Inquisitor
After each Swarm wave, gain an additional Blessing selection (choose 2 Blessings per Swarm wave instead of 1). Hero spawn cost +2 gold.
*Play: double blessings make you powerful fast — but building your army is expensive. You'll have incredible tools on a skeleton crew.*

### Oath of Judgment
Gain 1 Bronze Hero Tier Enhancer immediately. After Act 1, gain 1 Silver Hero Tier Enhancer. After Act 2 and each Act after, gain 1 Gold Hero Tier Enhancer. Blessing screens offer 1 fewer choice (2 instead of 3).
*Play: free tier power that escalates, but fewer blessing options. You're locked into whatever blessings appear. Build around tier advantage, not blessing stacking.*

### Oath of the Seeker
Hero recruitment always includes at least 1 hero matching your most active synergy. Each time you activate a new synergy threshold during the run, gain +5 gold and 1 random artifact.
*Play: chase synergy breakpoints aggressively — each new threshold funds the next one. The recruitment guarantee helps you get there, and every threshold you unlock pays out. Build wide synergy coverage, not tall stacks.*

### Oath of Devotion
All Wisdom scaling increased by +35%. All Strength scaling reduced by 25%. All allies gain +10% Max Mana.
*Play: physical carries are crippled. Build caster-heavy — but even your casters are slightly slower to cast. You need mana generation to compensate.*

### Oath of Purification
Blessing screens offer 1 additional choice (4 total). After each Blessing selection, gain 1 random synergy token. All allies start battle with -8% to all stats.
*Play: more choices, more synergies, but your team is permanently weaker in raw combat. You need the blessings and synergies to compensate for the stat deficit.*

## NEBULAE
*Identity: Alien, void, debuffs, penetration, corruption, entropy.*
*Playstyle: embrace randomness, corrupt everything, turn chaos into advantage.*

### Oath of Corrosion
When you equip an item, there is a 30% chance that item permanently gains +50% to all its existing stat bonuses. There is a 10% chance the item is destroyed instead.
*Play: every equip is a gamble. You'll get incredible items — and lose some in the process. Hoard extras, equip fearlessly.*

### Oath of the Void
All allies ignore 30% of the target's Physical Defense and Spell Defense when dealing damage. All allies start battle with -15% Max HP.
*Play: you shred tanky enemies, but you're fragile. Build offense-heavy and kill before they kill you.*

### Oath of Mutation
Each time you recruit a hero, they permanently mutate: one random stat increased by +30%, one different random stat reduced by -20%. 15% chance the mutated heroes spawn at Tier 2 instead of Tier 1.
*Play: every hero is warped. Some mutations are incredible, some are crippling. You must evaluate each mutant and decide: build around the buff or sell the dud. No hero arrives "normal."*

### Oath of Entropy
At the start of each preparation phase, hero spawn cost is randomly set to either 1 gold or 5 gold. Gain 1 additional free reroll on hero recruitment screens.
*Play: some phases you spawn for almost nothing, some phases spawning is crippling. When the Void gives you 1g spawns — go wild, fill your bench, merge everything. When it says 5g — hold, sell, wait. Every preparation phase is a different puzzle.*

### Oath of the Abyss
+1 Deploy Slot (4 at run start). Each wave, 2 random deployed heroes permanently gain +8% to a random stat. 1 random deployed hero permanently loses -10% to a random stat.
*Play: your board mutates over time. Heroes become lopsided — incredible in some stats, ruined in others. Adapt positioning around what the Abyss creates.*

## NEXUS
*Identity: Robotic, systematic, mana-driven, calculated efficiency, network synergy.*
*Playstyle: optimize systems, invest in items and synergies, sacrifice shortcuts for long-term efficiency.*

### Oath of Protocol
All item stat bonuses are 35% stronger. Hero spawn cost +1 gold.
*Play: items are everything — each one is dramatically more powerful. But building your army is expensive. Invest in items before heroes.*

### Oath of the Network
Each active synergy threshold grants +2 bonus gold per wave (max +10 per wave total). Starting Deploy Slot count reduced by 1 (2 at run start).
*Play: rush synergies with fewer heroes. You start crippled but each synergy threshold literally pays you. Wide synergy spread over tall stacking.*

### Oath of Fabrication
Start the run with 3 random basic items on the bench. After each Act, gain 2 additional random basic items. Blessing screens offer 1 fewer choice (2 instead of 3).
*Play: items rain down constantly but blessings are scarce. Your power comes from equipment, not divine favor. Itemize aggressively.*

### Oath of Efficiency
All gold gains reduced by 30%. Deploy Slot increases cost 50% less (rounded down). All hero spawn have a 25% chance to grant +1 additional Tier. At the end of the run, gain +50% bonus Valor, +30% bonus Hero Medals, and 2 Equipment Chests (rarity based on highest wave reached).
*Play: gold-starved but everything else is optimized — cheaper slots, stronger starting heroes. Every coin is precious. Push as far as possible for the massive end-of-run payout.*

### Oath of Integration
+2 Deploy Slots (5 at run start). All deployed heroes gain +2% to all stats per active synergy threshold on your team. All allies lose -3% to all stats for each deployed hero with no active synergy.
*Play: synergy is mandatory. Every hero without synergy weakens the whole team. Build tight, synergy-focused compositions — no dead weight allowed.*

## SHOGUNATE
*Identity: Speed, critical strikes, aggression, honor, blade mastery.*
*Playstyle: kill fast, play clean, punish yourself for failure.*

### Oath of the Blade
All allies gain +35% Crit Damage. All allies start battle with -20% Max HP.
*Play: massive crits on a fragile team. If your damage doesn't delete enemies fast, you'll fold. Build crit-stacking carries and protect them.*

### Oath of Swiftness
If you win a battle before Overdrive begins (within 30 seconds), gain +5 gold and 1 random basic item. If combat reaches Overdrive, lose 3 gold.
*Play: you are PUNISHED for slow fights. Build fast, aggressive comps that end battles quickly. Tanky stall strategies are actively harmful.*

### Oath of Bushido
Gain +1 bonus gold for each ally that survives the battle. Losing a wave grants 0 gold instead of the normal 50% reward.
*Play: flawless victories are massively profitable. Sloppy wins and losses are financially devastating. Position perfectly, protect everyone.*

### Oath of the Ronin
Start the run with 1 fewer Deploy Slot (2 at run start). Heroes gain +25% to all stats when they are the only hero of their faction currently deployed on the battlefield.
*Play: one hero per faction. Your board is a mosaic of factions — each hero is powerful alone but you sacrifice faction synergy thresholds entirely. Multi-faction, class-synergy focused builds.*

### Oath of Honor
The first ally to land a killing blow each battle permanently gains +3% Strength and +3% Crit Damage (stacks across waves, no cap). All allies start battle with -10% Max HP.
*Play: one hero becomes your champion, growing infinitely. Position your carry to always strike first. The rest of the team exists to set up the champion's kills.*
