Synergies are the core composition layer of Strifeborn. They reward long-term planning and coherent team construction by granting escalating bonuses when you field enough heroes of the same faction, class, or specialized archetype.

All synergies are **fully visible**, **deterministic in activation**, and **never disabled once active**. There are no hidden synergies and no temporary deactivation mid-combat.

---

## Three Synergy Types

Strifeborn uses exactly three synergy types:

1. **Faction Synergies** — thematic identity and long-term combat behavior
2. **Class Synergies** — functional combat roles and immediate impact
3. **Extra Synergies** — specialized identity extensions tied to specific heroes

---

## Faction Synergies

Each faction synergy activates at **2 / 4 / 6 units**. All unlocked thresholds stack additively — reaching Level II keeps Level I active.

Any class can belong to any faction. Faction synergies are designed to benefit all class archetypes equally.

### Aegis (2/4/6)
*Identity: Defensive ramp through shielding and sustained protection.*

- **I (2):** Fortified Start — allies start battle with Shield = 5% Max HP per adjacent ally.
- **II (4):** Grinding Advance — every 2s, grant +2% Damage Reduction (stacks 10, persists all battle).
- **III (6):** War Machine — each 1s, allies gain +1% Damage Amp and +10 Phys/Spell Def (stacks 20, persists all battle).

### Eclipse (2/4/6)
*Identity: Death fuels the living. Every death makes the team stronger.*

- **I (2):** Resonant Passing — on any unit death, allies +3% Strength & +3% Wisdom (stacks 10, persists all battle).
- **II (4):** Tithe of the Fallen — on takedown, killer heals 8% Max HP and gains +10 Attack Speed for 3s.
- **III (6):** Cycle Unbroken — the first time each ally would die, they revive at 25% HP after 1s and gain +25% Damage Amp for 3s (once per ally).

### Inquisition (2/4/6)
*Identity: Judgment through Marks. Wisdom-driven escalation.*

- **I (2):** Acolyte's Insight — team +20% Wisdom, +20% HP.
- **II (4):** Edict of Judgement — at start and every 4s, Mark the highest-HP enemy; −15% Damage Reduction to Marked enemy (stacks 2, persists all battle).
- **III (6):** Doctrinal Surge — when allies cast, gain +1% Strength and +1% Wisdom (stacks 40, persists all battle); hitting a Marked target grants +25% Mana Regen for 2s.

### Nebulae (2/4/6)
*Identity: Corruption through debuffs. Penetration and scaling from battlefield pollution.*

- **I (2):** Non-Euclid — for each active debuff, allies gain +2% Spell Pen & +2% Phys Pen (stacks 10).
- **II (4):** Gravity Pulse — every 8s, deal 12% of each enemy's Missing HP as Spell, then apply −15% Attack Speed (4s) to enemies hit.
- **III (6):** Inversion — for each active debuff, allies gain +1% Crit and +5% Crit Damage (stacks 10).

### Nexus (2/4/6)
*Identity: Mana network. Spells fuel the system.*

- **I (2):** Machine Built — team +15% HP, +25% Phys/Spell Def.
- **II (4):** Quick Start — allies start battle with +50% Max Mana (rounded up).
- **III (6):** Audit Beam — whenever any ally casts, gain +10 Mana.

### Shogunate (2/4/6)
*Identity: Crit-driven aggression. Guaranteed crits fuel scaling.*

- **I (2):** Opening Kata — allies start battle with +20% Move Speed and the first hit against a new target is a guaranteed Crit.
- **II (4):** Between Moments — each ally Critical Hit heals 15% of the damage done and grants +2.5% Attack Speed (stacks 20, persists all battle).
- **III (6):** Perfect Cycle — for each ally Critical Hit, allies gain +1% Strength, +1% Wisdom, +1% Crit (stacks 30, persists all battle).

## Class Synergies

Each class synergy activates at **2 / 4 / 6 units**.

### Fighter (2/4/6)
*Identity: Sustained brawler who ramps through combat.*

- **I (2):** Brawl Line — on hit, gain +2% Strength and +1% Lifesteal (stacks 10, persists all battle).
- **II (4):** Momentum — on kill, gain Shield for 100% Strength or 100% Wisdom (whichever is higher).
- **III (6):** Punishing Guard — after getting hit 5 times, gain +15% Counter and +12% Damage Reduction for 2s.

### Tank (2/4/6)
*Identity: Frontline anchor who protects allies and grows tougher.*

- **I (2):** Stand Ground — +50 Phys Def & +50 Spell Def per Tank hero.
- **II (4):** Bulwark Engine — when falling below 40% HP, gain Shield = 12% Max HP and +10% Reflect.
- **III (6):** Hold Forever — each time a Tank dies, share 50% of that Tank's Phys Def & Spell Def to other Tanks.

### Support (2/4/6)
*Identity: Team amplifier whose casts empower everyone.*

- **I (2):** Opening Tune-up — +20% Healing & Shielding; allies gain +10% Strength & +10% Wisdom (persists all battle).
- **II (4):** Rotation Care — when a Support casts, all allies gain +25 Phys Def & +25 Spell Def (stacks 8 times, persists all battle).
- **III (6):** Rhythm of Aid — when a Support casts, all allies gain +2% Damage Amp & +2% Damage Reduction (stacks 10 times, persists all battle).

### Shooter (2/4/6)
*Identity: Ranged damage dealer who shreds from distance.*

- **I (2):** Sighted In — +1 Range and Basic Attacks have +10% chance to fire an extra hit for 60% STR Physical (rolls Crit; same target).
- **II (4):** Piercing Lines — Basic Attacks ignore 18% of the target's Phys Def & Spell Def.
- **III (6):** Cadence — every 3rd Shooter Basic Attack deals bonus 12% missing HP.

### Assassin (2/4/6)
*Identity: Backline diver who chains kills through stealth.*

- **I (2):** Backline Leap — at battle start, Assassins jump to the enemy backline and gain Stealth for 1.5s.
- **II (4):** Cut Window — gain +18% Damage Amp to enemies below 30% HP.
- **III (6):** Ambush Loop — kills grant Stealth for 1s; when breaking Stealth, gain +20% Crit and +40% Crit Damage for 2s.

### Caster (2/4/6)
*Identity: Spell-driven powerhouse who escalates through casting.*

- **I (2):** Spell Prep — your first Spell this battle gains +15% Wisdom and +10% Spell Pen.
- **II (4):** Arcane Weave — after casting, your next Basic Attack deals +50% WIS as Spell.
- **III (6):** Echo — every 2nd Spell Echoes at 60% effectiveness (Echoes cannot Echo).

## Extra Synergies

Extra synergies are **specialized identity extensions** that originate from specific heroes. They activate at **1 / 2 / 3** and scale a single effect in intensity (rather than adding separate effects at each threshold).

Heroes themselves do not directly contribute +1 to an Extra synergy counter by being on the field. Instead, each hero *defines* one Extra synergy that enters the run's pool. Extra synergy counters are accumulated through **Artifacts** (equipped on battlefield heroes) and **Tokens** (granted by blessings and effects).

### Notable Extra Synergies

**Anchor** (GRV-T, Nexus Tank) — frontline allies gain massive Phys & Spell Def; adjacent allies gain half. Scales from +80/80 at rank 1 to +160/160 at rank 3.

**Artillery** (Brakka, Aegis Shooter) — every 5th Basic Attack explodes in a 3×3 area for Physical damage. Scales from 120% STR at rank 1 to 235% STR at rank 3.

**Berserker** (Barkhul, Eclipse Fighter) — while below 50% HP, bonus Strength and Lifesteal. Scales from +15% Str/+10% LS to +35% Str/+20% LS.

**Reaper** (Lunara, Eclipse Assassin) — bonus damage to enemies below 50% HP; heals on kill. Scales from +7% damage/4% heal to +21% damage/10% heal.

**Singularity** (Ael'Yzeth, Nebulae Caster) — every 15s, pulse Missing HP as Spell to all enemies. Scales from 15% at rank 1 to 35% at rank 3.

**Voidweaver** (Vema, Eclipse Caster) — Spell Crit Chance and Crit Damage. Scales from +15% Crit/+30% Crit Dmg to +35% Crit/+70% Crit Dmg.

**Wraith** (Kazuro, Shogunate Assassin) — Dodge after casting; heals when dodging. Scales from +5% Dodge/2% heal to +15% Dodge/6% heal.

There are 35 Extra synergies in total, each tied to one of the 42 heroes in the roster.

---

## Synergy Sources at a Glance

| Source | What it contributes |
|---|---|
| Hero on battlefield | +1 to their Faction synergy; +1 to their Class synergy |
| Artifact (equipped) | +1 to a specific synergy (Faction, Class, or Extra) |
| Token (blessing/effect) | +1 to a specific synergy counter; no equip required |

Heroes never activate Extra synergies by presence alone — only Artifacts and Tokens can push Extra synergy counters.
